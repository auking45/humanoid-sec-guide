# 4.1.6. Data Protection Checklist

The Data Protection Checklist is for ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of data transmitted and received by the robot. It includes encryption, key management, and protection of sensitive data, aiming to prevent data eavesdropping and manipulation.

| ID | Security Level | Checklist Item | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| DP-01 | L1 | Confidentiality of<br>information | The capability to protect the confidentiality of information stored in storage and transmitted over communication channels to prevent unauthorized disclosure shall be provided. |
| DP-02 | L2 | Data deletion management | When an active service is decommissioned or disposed of, the capability to delete information stored in all non-volatile information storage that supports clear read access shall be provided. |
| DP-03 | L1 | Use of secure<br>cryptographic mechanisms | When encryption is required, internationally recognized secure cryptographic mechanisms shall be used. |
| DP-04 | L1 | Personal data protection | Legal requirements related to personal data shall be complied with when collecting and processing personal data. |
